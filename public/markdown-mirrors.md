## Site Summary

EverythingUTM is a student-built web app for Universiti Teknologi Malaysia students. It brings important campus workflows into one place: marketplace buying and selling, campus map discovery, chats, Q&A, past papers, transportation requests, bus schedules, profiles, settings, privacy, and bug reporting.

Primary site: https://everythingutm.netlify.app/

## What EverythingUTM Is For

EverythingUTM is designed to reduce friction for UTM students by putting common student needs into one portal. Instead of checking separate chats, maps, files, sellers, transport contacts, and notes, students can open one app and move between practical campus tools.

## Feature Details

### Marketplace

The marketplace lets students post listings, browse items, filter by category, favourite listings, view seller information, open listing details, and handle payment preference information such as DuitNow QR, TnG wallet, bank transfer, or cash. Common categories include books, electronics, room items, transport, food, and services.

### Campus Map

The campus map helps students find important UTM and nearby locations. Categories include gates, academic places, student-life spots, food areas, residential colleges, sports facilities, services, and transport hubs. It is useful for finding pickup points, campus buildings, food places, and nearby student errands.

### Chats

Campus chats are organized into channels such as General, Lost & Found, Assignments, Food, Roommates, and Events. The chat system supports text conversations and community interaction between UTM students.

### Q&A

The Q&A section is for questions about classes, assignments, campus life, and student help. Students can post questions, answer questions, vote or mark helpful responses, and attach supporting content.

### Past Papers

The past papers area helps students upload, organize, and find study materials by faculty, year, semester, and test type. It can also link students to community drive folders for academic resources.

### Transportation

The transportation section supports ride and delivery requests. Students can enter pickup and drop-off places, schedule, budget, notes, and payment preference. Requests can move through statuses such as open, matched, paid, or completed.

### Bus Schedule

The bus schedule section provides UTM shuttle timetable information, including regular campus shuttle schedules and special BDR Tuesday/Thursday schedules. It includes route codes, route descriptions, stop directions, page images, and notes.

### Our Family

The Our Family section is a community gallery/profile area showing members of the EverythingUTM community. It helps students recognize users, view profile information, and build trust.

### Marketplace Reels

Marketplace Reels is a visual browsing mode for featured marketplace listings. It is designed for quick discovery and a more social shopping experience.

### Profile

The profile area lets a user set up their identity in the app, including username, name, role, faculty, matric number, contact number, study year, profile picture, wallet, and reviews.

### Settings and Support

Settings includes appearance controls, language choices, privacy policy, help center, bug reporting, authentication controls, payment/provider settings, and account deletion.

## Important Limitations

EverythingUTM is not the official Universiti Teknologi Malaysia website. Dynamic listings, chats, requests, profiles, and user-generated content may change over time. Official policies, university announcements, and sudden bus schedule changes should be verified through official UTM channels.
